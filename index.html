About Us

Dedicated to Social Welfare & Community Building

Pratham Vijay Janseva Charitable Trust is a non-profit organization committed to uplifting the underprivileged sections of society. Founded with a vision of equality and opportunity for all, we work tirelessly to bring positive change.

Our Mission

To empower marginalized communities through education, healthcare, and sustainable livelihood programs.

Our Vision

A society where every individual has access to basic rights, dignity, and opportunities to thrive.
